Password Reset

A password reset is needed for newly created WiSecure accounts. To perform password reset follow the steps below.

Step One

Click on "Forgot Password?" link from the Login page.

Step Two

Enter your WiSecure email account and click on the Request Code Button.

*Note: This will send a 6 digit code on the mobile number that is associated with your WiSecure account.

Step Three

Enter the 6 digit code number that you receive through your mobile number.

Step Four

Enter your new Password. Click the Reset Password button to save your new password.

***WiSecure recommends to use a strong password consists of 8 or more characters in length and a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers and special symbols (e.g. *, _, $, &).
