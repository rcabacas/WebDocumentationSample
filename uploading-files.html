Uploading Files

There are two ways to upload files on WiSecure.

Method 1: Browsing for the files to upload.

Step One

Click on the Upload Files button on the lower left part of your screen.

A File Explorer window will popup. Navigate to your file folders and select the file or files that you want to upload.

Step Two

Click the Open button on the File Explorer window to upload the files.

Method 2: Drag and Drop files to upload.

Step One

Open the Location Folder of your files.

Step Two

From the location of your files, select the file or files and Drag and Drop them to the Upload Files button on the lower left part of your dashboard.

After performing either of the two methods described above, an upload progress percentage will show on the Upload Files button.

An upload progress animation will also show beside the Issue Ticket icon indicating the loading progress of the file upload.

A Load Complete message will appear on the leftmost column of your WiSecure Dashboard indicating a successful and completed upload.

Now you are ready to select the schema and compile your files. Click here to proceed.
